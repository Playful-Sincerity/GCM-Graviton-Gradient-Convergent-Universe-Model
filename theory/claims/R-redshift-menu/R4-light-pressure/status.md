# R4 — Status

**Epistemic tier:** 🔴 Red

**One-line status:** Under-specified. No quantitative pressure-frequency relation. Distinguishing feature from R2 is unclear. Retained in menu for conceptual completeness but not a candidate for primary commitment.

## Why Red

- No quantitative form $\nu = f(\rho_g)$ derived from GCM dynamics.
- No clear distinction from R2 (void decompression) as currently specified.
- Classical tired-light failure mode applies unless paired with R6 (time dilation) — which makes R4 structurally similar to R1 + R6.
- CMB blackbody preservation would require frequency-independence, which has no a priori justification for a pressure coupling.

## What Would Promote R4 to Yellow

1. **Specify the pressure-frequency relation.** Derive $\nu = f(\rho_g)$ from GCM dynamics — ideally from the minimal-model equation of motion applied to a photon-cluster in an ambient graviton substrate.
2. **Distinguish from R2.** Show clearly what R4 predicts that R2 does not, and test that prediction against data.
3. **Address CMB blackbody.** Show that the coupling is frequency-independent to FIRAS precision or that any frequency dependence mimics adiabatic cooling.
4. **Produce a distance-redshift relation.** Show what $z(d)$ R4 predicts and compare to $H_0 d / c$ at low $z$.

## What Would Demote R4 Further

There is no "further" below red for R4 as specified. The mechanism is currently too vague to falsify in any rigorous sense. It is included in the menu because *some* form of photon-substrate coupling is expected in GCM, but as a primary redshift mechanism it has no standing.

## Comparison to Peer-Reviewed Alternatives

No direct peer-reviewed analog. Concepts vaguely in the neighborhood:

- **Spectroscopic interactions in plasma:** photons traversing plasma do acquire frequency-dependent dispersion, and this is observationally measurable (pulsar timing, FRB dispersion measures). R4 would need to show it produces a non-plasma-like shift that survives at cosmological distances.
- **Space refractive-index variation:** some speculative cosmological models (conformal cosmology, varying-$c$) propose that the effective medium of space has a non-constant optical index. These are controversial and do not directly correspond to R4.

## Honest Flag

R4 as presented is a placeholder. It is the weakest mechanism in the menu. If Wisdom is willing to drop R4 entirely, the menu becomes R1, R2, R3, R5 + R6 companion — a cleaner presentation. R4 is retained here in case a future refinement of GCM produces a specific pressure-frequency coupling that deserves its own name.

**Recommendation for the coherence paper:** mention R4 in a single sentence ("photon-substrate pressure coupling as a possible residual effect") rather than giving it a full subsection. The other menu mechanisms carry the structural weight.
