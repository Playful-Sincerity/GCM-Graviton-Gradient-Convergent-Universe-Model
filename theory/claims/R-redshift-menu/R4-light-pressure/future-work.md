# R4 — Future Work / Paper-1 Status

**Paper-1 decision:** EXCLUDE, or single-line mention only. Do not include in R-menu presentation.
**Source:** `theory/claims/audit-2026-04-24.md` (R4 row).

## Why excluded

R4 is explicitly a "placeholder mechanism" with **no mathematical body.** Claim.md states:
- No derived $\nu = f(\rho_g)$ functional form
- Proposed $\nu_{\text{obs}} \propto \rho_g^k$ for unspecified exponent $k$
- No explanation for why cosmological photons arrive redshifted rather than simply shifting as they pass through voids

Including R4 as a menu item in paper-1 risks diluting the menu's credibility. The other R mechanisms (R1, R2, R5) have at least structural/dimensional content; R4 has a name and an intuition.

## What would promote R4 to INCLUDE

1. **Specify a microscopic mechanism** for how ambient graviton "pressure" modulates photon frequency. Options: scattering cross-section that depends on local $\rho_g$; emergent refractive index from substrate density; coupling to graviton substrate oscillation modes.
2. **Derive the exponent $k$** from GCM dynamics, not parameter fitting.
3. **Reconcile with the "pressure reads at observer" claim.** R4 distinguishes itself from R2 by saying "frequency is set by current ambient pressure, not accumulated transit." Needs a specific mechanism for how a photon's frequency is *re-set* by each region it enters (or by the observer's location).

None of these have been done. R4 remains intuition-without-formalism.
