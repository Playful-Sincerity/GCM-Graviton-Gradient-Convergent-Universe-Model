## Why deferred

$N \sim 10^{185}$ is just $V_{\text{Hubble}} / \ell_P^3$ — sphere volume divided by cube volume. mpmath and Wolfram both correctly compute this to 14 digits. The agreement confirms standard geometry, not anything about GCM.

The load-bearing GCM-specific assumption — **"one graviton per Planck cell"** — is OQ-4 (vacuum graviton density), explicitly open. M3's number inherits this unverified assumption. Absent OQ-4's resolution, the Planck-cell count is a geometric exercise with a GCM interpretive label.

Including M3 in the "verified math spine" risks the same padding perception as E7 and Q3 — inflating the count of "verified claims" with items that verify textbook calculation, not GCM physics.

## What would promote M3 to INCLUDE

1. **Resolve OQ-4.** Derive vacuum graviton density $\rho_0$ from first principles (what's the zero-point density of the graviton substrate?). Once $\rho_0$ is derived, $N = V \rho_0$ becomes a real GCM-specific computation.
