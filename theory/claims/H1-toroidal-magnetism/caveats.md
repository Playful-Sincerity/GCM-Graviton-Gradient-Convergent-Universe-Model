# H1 — Toroidal Magnetism: Caveats

**Claim code:** H1
**Date:** 2026-04-23

---

## What H1 Establishes

- A structural geometric mechanism (toroidal oscillation of graviton density around the motion axis of a charge) that produces the qualitative character of magnetic fields
- Natural emergence of the right-hand rule from the geometry
- Topological consistency with the empirical absence of magnetic monopoles
- A parsimonious fit within GCM's ontology (configurational, not graviton-intrinsic — consistent with the Axiom of Indivisibility)

## What H1 Does NOT Establish

### UNSPEC-4 Remains Open

The Lorentz force $F = q(\mathbf{E} + \mathbf{v}\times\mathbf{B})$ is verified to roughly parts in $10^{15}$ in penning-trap precision experiments. GCM's H1 sketch does not reproduce this — not the functional form, not the magnitude, not the velocity dependence. Closing UNSPEC-4 would require:

1. A formal wave equation for toroidal graviton-density oscillations
2. A derivation showing that two such oscillations, one stationary and one moving, produce a force with the exact $\mathbf{v}\times\mathbf{B}$ structure
3. A derivation of the electromagnetic coupling constants from graviton-substrate parameters

None of these exist. UNSPEC-4 is recognized as a substantial open problem; it is treated in S8 (Structural Challenges) at medium severity (well-specified direction, non-trivial execution).

### No Maxwell's Equations

The four Maxwell equations in their standard form have no GCM derivation. In particular:
- Gauss's law for $\mathbf{E}$: GCM would need to show that $\nabla\cdot\mathbf{E} = \rho/\epsilon_0$ emerges from $\nabla\cdot J_g$ being the charge proxy
- Gauss's law for $\mathbf{B}$ ($\nabla\cdot\mathbf{B} = 0$): Qualitatively consistent with monopole absence, but no formal derivation
- Faraday's law ($\nabla\times\mathbf{E} = -\partial\mathbf{B}/\partial t$): No GCM derivation
- Ampère-Maxwell law: No GCM derivation

The quantitative structure of electromagnetism is not yet available in GCM. H1 is one brick in what would have to become a much larger structure.

### No Value of $c$ from Graviton Dynamics

Special relativity (S5.5(b)) assumes gravitons propagate effects at $c$. GCM does not derive $c$ from graviton properties. Maxwell's equations in standard theory have $c^2 = 1/(\epsilon_0\mu_0)$; GCM has no analog of this relation yet. This connects to OQ-3 (Lorentz invariance emergence) — closing UNSPEC-4 quantitatively would likely require progress on OQ-3 at the same time.

### No Value of $e$ from Flow Asymmetry Strength

GCM claims charge is quantized because any sub-threshold excess is "shed as light before accumulating to a full unit" (v2 H2). The specific value of the elementary charge $e = 1.602 \times 10^{-19}$ C has no GCM derivation. A full theory would have to predict this, or at least show that it is parametrized by graviton-substrate properties in a consistent way.

### Preon Failure Modes Inherited

GCM is structurally a preon program — gravitons play the role of preons composing electrons, quarks, photons. Preon programs have historically struggled with:
- Hypercolor confinement: why don't we see graviton-scale degrees of freedom at LHC energies?
- Wrong masses: why do composite particles have the specific masses they do?

H1 does not address these. They belong to S8 and to future work.

## How H1 Becomes Green

H1 would move to green tier if:
1. A formal wave equation for toroidal graviton-density oscillations is written and its solutions characterized
2. That wave equation, combined with the flow-asymmetry picture of charge, produces something isomorphic to Maxwell's equations in a limit
3. The resulting theory matches at least one precision electromagnetic measurement (fine structure constant, electron anomalous magnetic moment) quantitatively

Each of these is a substantial theoretical project — together, they are the theory of electromagnetism that GCM would need to build. Expected to remain yellow-tier for the coherence paper's horizon.

## Honest Framing for the Coherence Paper

In the coherence paper's S7, H1 will be stated as:

> GCM proposes that magnetic fields arise from coherent toroidal oscillations of graviton density in configurations of moving charge. The qualitative geometry — including the right-hand rule and the absence of monopoles — is consistent with this picture. The exact reproduction of the Lorentz force and of Maxwell's equations remains an open problem (UNSPEC-4). This is treated in Section S8 as a medium-severity structural challenge: the direction for future work is well-specified, but substantial theoretical development is required.

That framing is the bar for the coherence case. Nothing more is claimed.
