# L3 — Photon Wavelength Proportional to Graviton-Cluster Spacing

**Section:** S5 (Light and Photon Physics)
**Claim code:** L3
**Epistemic tier:** 🟡 Yellow — dimensional argument is clean; mechanism is a reframing of wave theory, not a novel derivation. The claim is aspirational in the sense that it attributes standard wave geometry to a specific substrate without deriving that attribution.
**Session:** E
**Date:** 2026-04-23

---

## The Claim

A photon is a traveling density-structure of gravitons. Within this structure, there exist regions of high graviton density (the wave's crests) and regions of lower density (the troughs). The photon's wavelength $\lambda$ is proportional to the spacing $d$ between successive high-density regions:

$$\lambda \propto d$$

More specifically, $\lambda = N \cdot d$ where $N$ is the number of cluster spacings per wavelength. If $N = 1$ (one cluster per wavelength), then $\lambda = d$ exactly.

**Consequence for redshift:** As a photon redshifts — its wavelength increasing — the graviton-cluster spacing within the photon's internal structure also increases proportionally. This is not a coincidence; in GCM, the wavelength IS the cluster spacing (or a fixed multiple of it).

---

## GCM Motivation

In standard EM theory, a photon's wavelength is the characteristic length scale of its electromagnetic wave. This wave is described mathematically as an oscillating field. GCM's move is to interpret this wave structure physically: the electromagnetic oscillation is a real oscillation in graviton density, and the characteristic length scale (the wavelength) is the characteristic spacing between density peaks.

This is a **reframing**, not a new derivation. The same $\lambda$ that appears in $E = hc/\lambda$ is the same $\lambda$ that appears in $d \sim \lambda$. The claim is that $d$ and $\lambda$ refer to the same underlying physical structure, viewed from different theoretical frameworks.

---

## What This Claim Does and Does Not Prove

**Does:** Propose a dimensionally consistent identification of photon wavelength with graviton-cluster spacing. Provide the mechanism that R6 (photon-structure time dilation) depends on. Give GCM a physical picture of what "a photon" is internally.

**Does not:** Derive the photon wave equation from graviton dynamics. Show from first principles that gravitons would arrange themselves in periodic density waves. Predict a specific relationship between $m_g$, $N$, and $\lambda$ (the $N$ factor is currently undetermined).

---

## Relation to Other Claims

- **R6 (photon-structure time dilation)** depends on L3. If L3 fails — if there is no clean relationship between wavelength and internal spacing — then R6's mechanism loses its grounding, and GCM must rely on timescape's mechanism (clock-rate structure) for time dilation.
- **L1 (photon mass)** is compatible with L3 but independent. Knowing the photon mass doesn't determine the cluster spacing; that requires knowing $m_g$ and $N$ separately.
- **L2 (photon speed)** is independent — L2 depends on the total mass, not the internal structure.
