## Current state

Red-tier conceptual only. Q4 commits GCM to a **realist** reading of wave-particle duality: $\psi$ is a projection onto a real distribution of graviton density structure, not a probability amplitude. Combined with locality and superdeterminism (CC-5.5, 't Hooft 2016), Q4 positions GCM on the local-realist-via-superdeterminism path.

No numerical content. No derivation of Schrödinger equation, Born rule, double-slit, entanglement, or QFT limit. Full treatment would require reformulating QM.

## Why INCLUDE in paper-1 (despite being red)

The realist + superdeterministic commitment is a **position the paper must state** for two reasons:
1. "Local realism" without qualification triggers instant dismissal on Bell grounds.
2. The local-realist-via-superdeterminism reading is a minority but defensible position (cited: 't Hooft's cellular automaton interpretation, Hossenfelder 2020+).

Two paragraphs, cited, with the explicit flag that *reformulating QM is out of scope for paper-1 and is research-program-level future work.* That is honest and fits the coherence-case standard.

## What would promote Q4 from conceptual to derivational

Derive Schrödinger equation from graviton-substrate dynamics. Derive Born rule as emergent measurement-apparent statistics over deterministic graviton microstates. Reproduce double-slit as real density structure passing through both slits. Reproduce Bell-violating correlations as common-history artifacts in the deterministic substrate flow. Recover QFT as a continuum-field limit.

Each of these is substantial (probably 100+ page papers in their own right). The full program is decades-scale. Paper-1 stakes the claim; papers 4-20 work on individual pieces.
