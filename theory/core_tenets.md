# GCM Core Tenets — Philosophical Framework
**Version**: 0.1 (Pre-formal)
**Date**: 2026-03-21

These are the non-negotiable axioms of the framework. Everything else is hypothesis, derivation, or conjecture that can change. These cannot.

---

## Tenet 1: One Fundamental Particle

There is exactly one type of fundamental particle. It is dimensionless (a point). Its properties only manifest through its combination with other such points. We call it the **graviton**.

All matter, energy, space, and time are configurations of gravitons.

*Implication*: Electrons, quarks, photons, protons — all are stable patterns in graviton density. The apparent diversity of particles is a diversity of configurations, not of substances.

---

## Tenet 2: Gravity is the Only Fundamental Force

There is exactly one fundamental force: **gravity** — the tendency of gravitons to attract one another.

All other forces (Electromagnetic, Strong, Weak) are emergent phenomena arising from gravitational interactions at different scales and densities.

*Implication*: Repulsion is not a fundamental force. What we observe as repulsion is **competing attraction** — a configuration where each object is more strongly attracted elsewhere than toward the other, creating a net separating effect in a particular reference frame or density context.

---

## Tenet 3: The Universe is Converging

The universe is governed by gravity at all scales. The long-term trajectory of the universe is **convergence** — matter and energy drawn together by gravity.

Current cosmological interpretation of accelerating expansion is incorrect — it reflects a misinterpretation of redshift and luminosity data, or an artifact of our position/perspective in the universe.

*Implication*: Dark energy, as currently conceived, does not exist. The cosmological constant reflects either a measurement artifact or an emergent graviton density gradient effect at cosmic scales.

---

## Tenet 4: All Existence is Dimensions of Change

The graviton substrate is in constant flux. What we experience as physical reality — matter, forces, space, time, consciousness — are all dimensions of change within this substrate.

*Implication*: The wave-particle duality of quantum mechanics reflects the real gradient nature of particles (not probability). A particle is a nucleated density gradient — it has a center, but its boundary is real and gradual, not probabilistic.

---

## Philosophical Grounding

These tenets emerge from and reinforce the Gravitationalist Ethos:

> "The Universe is coming together through the force of gravity. We are the universe. Our identities are at their fullest form when they include everything. The meaning of Life is therefore to connect with the universe and to contribute to its coming together."

The physics and the philosophy are coherent: if gravity is the fundamental organizing force, and if the universe is converging, then the trajectory of existence itself is toward connection. Consciousness, life, and love are not anomalies in a cold mechanical universe — they are local instantiations of the universe's fundamental tendency.

---

## What These Tenets Do NOT Specify (Yet)

- The specific mass or size of the graviton
- The exact mechanism by which density gradients produce different force laws
- The specific reinterpretation of cosmological redshift data
- The mathematical structure of the graviton field
- How consciousness arises from graviton configurations

These are open research questions, not axioms.
