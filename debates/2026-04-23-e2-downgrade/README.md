## Concrete recommendation (from synthesis)

1. **E2 moves to S8 at red-tier.** Full computation preserved. Framed as: "sign-correct toy model in asymmetric geometry; magnitude off by $10^{40}$; isotropy requires full substrate treatment (undone); three candidate amplification mechanisms (density-dependent $G$, non-linear graviton coupling, contact interactions) listed with their fatal problems; none committed."

2. **S3 shortens to ~1,200 words**, anchored by E4's regularization finding ($U(R\to 0) = -Gm^2/(\sigma\sqrt{\pi})$, bounded binding, no self-energy divergence) as the actual positive structural result. E1 appears as hypothesis-with-flow-equation. E3 appears as framing co-option.

3. **One-sentence forward-pointer in S3** references S8's E2 treatment, with the $10^{40}$ number visible and the geometry-dependence flagged, so the sign computation is not orphaned.

4. **Drop "structural compatibility" label.** Use plain language: "sign-correct toy; magnitude requires additional physics not yet specified; isotropy requires full substrate treatment not yet done."

5. **Do not commit to contact interactions or density-dependent $G$ at this stage.** The "speculative-mechanism" commit would require a $10^{40}$-tuned coupling with no principle — worse than the middle path, not better.
