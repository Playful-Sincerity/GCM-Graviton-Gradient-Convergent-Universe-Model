## 1. Direct Response

**On ISW falsifiability.** The ISW prediction is not a win for GCM — it's a direct prediction failure. The ISW effect requires potential wells that *decay* over time (dark energy overcoming collapse). GCM's converging universe predicts potential wells *deepen*, producing a negative ISW cross-correlation. The observed signal is positive. To rescue this, GCM would need a void-growth mechanism producing the exact same potential-well decay rate as ΛCDM — which is empirical mimicry, not explanatory economy.

**On the evolutionary analogy for the ethos.** This analogy sharpens the problem rather than solving it. When we say evolution "selects for" traits, we use shorthand for statistical regularities — no one claims blind differential reproduction prescribes that organisms *ought* to reproduce. GCM wants to move from "gravitons converge" to "we ought to connect and contribute." That normative step requires additional premises the physics cannot supply, no matter how character-rich the process.

**On GR/QFT inconsistency as motivation.** The inconsistency of existing frameworks does not validate any specific alternative. GCM currently has zero derived parameters and zero quantitative predictions different from existing theories — which is not a better epistemic position than a parameterized predictive theory with 19 free parameters.

## 2. Final Strongest Case

**The parity violation wall is structural.** The weak force couples exclusively to left-handed fermions — discretely and exactly. A density gradient is a scalar field with a vector gradient direction. Neither a scalar nor a vector gradient has a preferred handedness in three-dimensional space. Handedness requires a pseudovector or axial structure. This is not a quantitative gap; it is a vocabulary problem. The framework as stated cannot express the distinction between left and right-handed fermion coupling because its fundamental ingredient lacks the necessary mathematical structure.

**Bell and the competing-attraction mechanism.** "Competing attraction" is a locally-deterministic, realist picture of force. Bell's theorem (confirmed with all major loopholes closed by Zeilinger et al. 2022) demonstrates no locally-deterministic hidden variable theory can reproduce quantum correlations. If GCM's graviton configurations are hidden variables, they must be non-local. But graviton non-locality means instantaneous coordination across spacelike separations — either violating special relativity or requiring a preferred frame. The "competing attraction" picture is precisely the kind of locally-realistic mechanism Bell rules out.

**Zero derived parameters.** A framework is scientifically viable when it makes at least one quantitative prediction that differs from existing theories. GCM does not yet meet that bar. "Pre-formal" is honest, but it means the proposition — that GCM is *currently* scientifically viable — is premature.

## 3. Concessions

The cosmological case is more contested than I initially treated it. GCM's photon time-dilation mechanism is a non-trivial response to the tired-light objection — structurally coherent even if unquantified.

GR/QFT incompleteness is real motivation, not mere rhetoric.

The philosophical ethos has a more defensible "meaning from process" structure than I initially credited.

**The three structural barriers** — ISW positive correlation (prediction failure), Bell locality constraint (competing attraction is the exact mechanism Bell rules out), and maximal parity violation (requires mathematical structure the substrate lacks) — represent architectural mismatches that refinement cannot resolve without fundamental revision of the core tenets. These are not quantitative gaps awaiting numbers. They are what makes the current framework scientifically non-viable as stated, while the underlying program remains worth pursuing.
