# GCM Development Roadmap

## North Star
A fully rigorous, mathematically consistent, experimentally predictive unified physics theory — publishable in a peer-reviewed journal — derived from three core tenets.

---

## Phase 1: Philosophical Framework
**Goal**: A coherent, clearly stated conceptual system. Every claim is either a defined axiom or explicitly labeled as hypothesis. No math required yet, but no contradictions allowed.

**Deliverables**:
- [ ] Core tenets document (canonical, versioned)
- [ ] Glossary of GCM-specific terms
- [ ] Map of what is claimed vs. what is still open
- [ ] Statement of what "emergence" means in this context

**Blocking question**: Can repulsion-as-differential-attraction be stated precisely enough that it could in principle be falsified?

---

## Phase 2: Theoretical Document
**Goal**: Each claim is either derived or traceable to an axiom. Math is internally consistent (dimensional analysis passes, no circular reasoning). Known experimental results are either reproduced or explicitly deferred.

**Deliverables**:
- [ ] Dimensionally consistent equation system
- [ ] Derivation or sketch of: Coulomb scaling, Van der Waals, Pauli exclusion, energy quantization
- [ ] Cosmological model explaining redshift without accelerating expansion
- [ ] Statement of what GCM predicts differently from Standard Model + ΛCDM

**Blocking question**: What mechanism produces the different symmetries of the four forces from one substrate?

---

## Phase 3: Publishable Paper
**Goal**: Full academic paper. Every claim derived, prior literature cited, experiments proposed.

**Deliverables**:
- [ ] Full draft with abstract, introduction, theory, predictions, discussion
- [ ] Comparison to prior work (preon models, emergent gravity, etc.)
- [ ] At least 3 novel, testable experimental predictions
- [ ] Response to strongest objections

---

## Current State (2026-03-21)

### What's established (core tenets, non-negotiable)
- One fundamental particle (the graviton)
- Gravity is the only fundamental force
- Universe is converging, not expanding
- All existence is dimensions of change in the graviton substrate

### What's hypothesized (open to revision)
- Graviton mass derived from neutron density × Planck volume
- Neutron density as maximum density
- Charge as asymmetric graviton flow (electrons emit, protons absorb)
- Toroidal oscillation model of magnetism/spin
- Tetrahedral lattice of spacetime

### Known gaps to resolve
1. Dimensional consistency of mass/energy formulas (Section 6)
2. Symmetry problem: why do forces have different mathematical symmetries?
3. Parity violation: why is weak force left-handed only?
4. Quantitative fit of tetrahedral lattice to Bohr energy levels
5. Cosmological model: specific reinterpretation of Type Ia supernova data
